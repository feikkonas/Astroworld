what is astrology?

Astrology is the study about planets and stars positions, alignments, motions and their impact on human lives.
It is believed that the alignment of the sun, stars and planets at the time of birth,
contribute to their personality and even has an effect on relationships.

Most people already have an idea about astrology, even without knowing.
Zodiac signs and horoscopes are a good example of that.
